Support

Need help with SmartRecord?

For app support, bug reports, privacy questions, or App Store review inquiries, email us and include your macOS version, SmartRecord version, and a short description of what happened.

Email support mavdevsh@gmail.com

Recording permissions

If recording fails, open macOS System Settings and confirm that SmartRecord is allowed under Screen Recording and Microphone. You may need to quit and reopen SmartRecord after changing permissions.

Audio capture choices

SmartRecord can record microphone audio, system audio, both, or neither. Choose the mode before starting a recording so export and post-processing use the intended audio sources.

Export files

Exports are created as local video files. If a post-processing step fails, keep the project folder and contact support with the error message.
